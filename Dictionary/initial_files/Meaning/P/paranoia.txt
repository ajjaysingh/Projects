[36m         LONG  Paranoia is from the Greek para-, "beyond, irregular," and noos, "mind." It is an irregularity of the mind, a mental disorder that distorts a person's
               perception of what is real. Someone with paranoia may believe that other people are out to get them or are watching them all the time. Doctors
               who treat paranoia look for causes, and while patients can develop the disorder gradually and sometimes due to life circumstances, such as
               abuse or neglect, the use and abuse of drugs can also cause paranoia.[0m
