[36m         LONG  Estuary, pronounced "ES-choo-air-ee," comes from the Latin word aestuarium, meaning "a tidal marsh or opening." The calm waters of an estuary, where the mouth

               and animals. Chesapeake Bay and Puget Sound are estuaries.[0m
