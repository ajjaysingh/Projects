                                                                                     CONTRARY                                                                                     
  SHORT        Use contrary when two things are exactly opposite or entirely different. If something is contrary to something else, it is opposed to or against it.
  LONG         A contrary child is one who opposes people in authority. As a noun, the contrary means the opposite, as in "We will not give up; on the contrary, we will
               fight with all our power." The adjective contrary derives from Latin contrarius "opposite, opposed," from contra "against."
