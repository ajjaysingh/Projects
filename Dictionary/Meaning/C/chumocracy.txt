	Wiki Definition:
chumocracy
    - a ruling elite that is made up of people from the same background who went to the same schools and universities and know each other socially

    - government characterised by the appointment to office of friends of those in power

    - a derogatory term for a ruling elite that is made up of people from the same social background who went to the same schools and universities and know each other socially

e.g. If Clacton has problems, they are caused by the chumocracy in Westminster of which you are a part.
