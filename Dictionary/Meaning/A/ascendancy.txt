                                                                                    ASCENDANCY                                                                                    
  SHORT        When you assume ascendancy over someone else, you become more powerful than they are. Ascendancy is the state of being in a higher position.
  LONG         If you look at ascendancy, you see the word, ascend, which means to go up. Ascendancy is not so much about going up, but about being up. Another word for it
               is dominance, especially in the context of foreign or domestic relations. In school, if you question a teacher's ascendancy, you might be told
               to go to the principal's office. In this case, you're challenging the teacher's authority.
