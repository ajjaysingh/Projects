Amenable means willing to be responsive to; agreeable; able to submit to a higher authority.

Definition of amenable
    1 liable->(to be brought to account) :  answerable->(citizens amenable to the law)
    2   a :  capable of submission (as to judgment or test) :  suited->(The data is amenable to analysis.)
        b :  readily brought to yield, submit, or cooperate  eg. a government not amenable to change
        c :  willing e.g. was amenable to spending more time at home

Legal Definition of amenable

    2   a :  suited by nature an adult is not amenable to a juvenile treatment program
