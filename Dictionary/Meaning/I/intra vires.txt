	Wiki Definition:
              	Ultra vires  is a Latin phrase  meaning  "beyond the powers". If an act requires legal authority and it is done with such authority, it is characterised in
               law as  intra vires  ("within the powers"). If it is done without such authority, it is  ultra vires .
