[36m         LONG  The prefix in- in inequity means “not” or “opposite of.” Equity, means "justice or fairness." Put it together and you get inequity: something that’s unfair or
               unjust. The word can be used to describe a wide range of unfair situations, such as an inequity in a school's buying new equipment for the
               football team while the field hockey team continues to wear uniforms from 1981.[0m
