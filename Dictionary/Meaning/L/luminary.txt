                                                                                     LUMINARY                                                                                     
  SHORT        In scientific writing, Stephen J. Hawking is a luminary. People look up to this well-known scientist and author for his knowledge and insight.
  LONG         Although luminary can mean an object or celestial body that gives off light, you'll often hear people talk about parties at which there were many luminaries
               in attendance. In this case, luminary means celebrity or well-known person in sports or politics, science or the arts. Think of them as being
               bright lights that make a party sparkle.

	 Eg.  "One Of The Luminaries Of Child Psychiatry"
	 Eg.  "The Luminaries Of The Art World"
	 Synonyms:  leading light , inspiration , leader , expert , master , 
	 Antonyms:  nobody , pleb , 

 2   A Natural Light-Giving Body, Especially The Sun Or Moon.

 3   An Artificial Light.
