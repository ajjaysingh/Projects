[91m        SHORT  Do you have a friend who constantly turns the conversation to themselves, as in "That's great about your winning the award but do you think these pants look

[36m         LONG  In psychoanalysis, narcissism is erotic gratification derived from excessive love for yourself, and is considered to be a normal developmental stage. The
               reference is to Narcissus, a beautiful youth in Greek mythology who fell in love with his reflection in a body of water and was changed into
               the flower narcissus, which grows near water.[0m
