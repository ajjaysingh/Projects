[91m        SHORT  To renege is to go back on your word or fail to keep a promise.[0m
[36m         LONG  Not quite lying, reneging is more a sin of omission — failing to do what you said you would. The Latin negāre means "to deny," so by reneging on your word,
               you are denying someone whatever you promised them. In card games, you are said to renege if you play against the rules. To renege may be
               wrong, but it's not necessarily a punishable offense (unless you put that promise legally binding in writing). Still, it certainly doesn't make
               you look good![0m
