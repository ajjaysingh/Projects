                                                                                     REAKING                                                                                      

 1   Smell Strongly And Unpleasantly; Stink.
	 Eg.  "The Yard Reeked Of Wet Straw And Horse Manure"
	 Eg.  "The Whole Place Reeked Of Cheap Perfume"
	 Synonyms:  stink , smell , 

 2   Be Suggestive Of Something Unpleasant Or Undesirable.
	 Eg.  "The Speeches Reeked Of Anti-Semitism"

 3   Give Off Smoke, Steam, Or Fumes.
	 Eg.  "While Temples Crash, And Towers In Ashes Reek"
