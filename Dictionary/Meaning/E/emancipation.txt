                                                                                   EMANCIPATION                                                                                   
  SHORT        Emancipation is being set free from the control of someone or something. Your emancipation from your parents comes when you turn 18 and are legally considered
               an adult.
  LONG         The word appears most memorably in "the Emancipation Proclamation," Abraham Lincoln's order of 1863 that freed the slaves in the U.S. Emancipation can
               describe any kind of liberation: "If you're really chafing under the rigors of practicing for the upcoming game, you and your teammates can go
               on strike for emancipation from the grueling schedule your coach has decreed."
